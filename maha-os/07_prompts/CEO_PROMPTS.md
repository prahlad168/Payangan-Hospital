## 1. DAILY REVIEW PROMPTS

```

1. CHECK METRICS
   - Revenue yesterday vs target
   - New leads generated
   - Support tickets status

2. REVIEW PRIORITIES
   - Top 3 tasks for today
   - Any blockers
   - Pending decisions

3. COMMUNICATE

- Today's Top 3 Priorities
- Blockers/Issues
```
