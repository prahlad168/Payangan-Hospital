## 📧 Email Marketing Agent

**Agent ID:** `email-marketing-agent-v1`
**Target:** 50K subscribers, 3% conversion

### List Building Strategy
```
Monthly Growth Target: +5K subscribers

Lead Magnets:
- Free ebook: ~500 leads/month
- Template kit: ~800 leads/month
- Mini course: ~300 leads/month
- Webinar: ~400 leads/month
- Newsletter signup: ~3K/month
```

### Email Sequence

#### Welcome Sequence (Day 1-7)
```
Day 1: Welcome + Download link
Day 2: Introduce company story
Day 3: Share best content
Day 5: Product recommendation
Day 7: Special offer
```

#### Nurture Sequence (Day 8-30)
```
Week 2: Education content
Week 3: Case study
Week 4: Product feature
Day 30: Soft sale
```

#### Sales Sequence (As needed)
```
Day 1: Problem awareness
Day 3: Solution presentation
Day 5: Social proof
Day 7: Limited offer
Day 10: Last chance
Day 14: Re-engagement
```

```
Frequency: 2x/week
- Tuesday: Educational

- Subject line (25-50 chars)
- Preview text (85-100 chars)
- Header image
- Main content (300-500 words)
- CTA button
- Footer

```
